AI4GOOD Initiative Launched

The AI4GOOD event brought together several schools and universities from across Mauritius. The competition aimed to use AI to create videos from passages and poems taken from various selected writers. Participants were required to read and interpret these literary works creatively using only AI tools. It was a challenging yet exciting event that saw students put their AI skills to the test.
